# Writer Skill

Optional Copilot CLI skill that applies your writing voice to all text generation.

## Setup

Copy to your local Copilot skills directory:
- Windows: `%USERPROFILE%\.copilot\skills\writer\SKILL.md`
- macOS/Linux: `~/.copilot/skills/writer/SKILL.md`

## What to put here

Add your personal writing rules. Same content as `src/drafts/voice_profile.md`
but applies to everything Copilot CLI generates, not just the LinkedIn pipeline.

See the [Copilot CLI documentation](https://docs.github.com/en/copilot) for more on skills.
